Biography

I am a final-year PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science at UNC Chapel Hill (expected Apr 2026), working on multimodal sensing and generative modeling. My research targets robust perception under multipath/occlusion and device heterogeneity, spanning audio, mmWave radar, WiFi CSI, and RGB-D.

Representative formulation: learn z = f_θ(x_audio, x_mmWave, x_CSI, x_vision), then solve tasks with y = g_ϕ(z). I aim for systems that are not only accurate but also reproducible and deployable.
